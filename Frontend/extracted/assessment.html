C · Scoring system

Strict Logic. Computed, Never Granted.

Individual certification

0%50%100%

Overall score ≥ 80% → Certified Individual. 79.9% is not certified. There is no rounding up and no manual override.

Organisation readiness

0%50%100%

95% of all employees must each score 80%+ for the organisation to become POSH Ready. Recomputed after every submission.

Output states

✕ Not Certified

Below 80%. Re-training triggered; re-attempt scheduled; readiness unaffected.

✓ Certified Individual

80%+. Digital certificate issued with evidence trail; counts toward the 95% rule.

★ Organisation POSH Ready

95% rule met. Status recorded, dated, and audit booking unlocked.

D · Evidence system

Every Submission Leaves a Trail

No screenshots, no attendance sheets. Five fields are generated for every single submission, automatically.

1 · TimestampISO-8601 submission time, plus per-question elapsed time.
2 · Response traceThe exact option or text entered, in order, with edits recorded.
3 · Score breakdownPoints earned per format: knowledge, recall, judgment, decisions.
4 · Audit log referenceAn immutable reference (e.g. AUD-LOG-2026-8C41F2A0) that the auditor resolves during verification.
